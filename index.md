This is the HIS 337 class website for the student final projects using [Xanthan framework](https://xanthan-web.github.io/xanthan/). Everything you see here is a placeholder — the title above, the essay topics, the images. 

This opening section will introduce your topic and lay out your argument about why your topic is significant for understanding U.S. society between 1945-2000s. Remember that all of your webpage content will need to support and develop this argument in some way. Because a webpage communicates information in sections or more digestible portions, you will want to consider how each section or portion below develops a particular point of your argument.
